ABOUT TETRAX

TETRAX launched operations in Lebanon in 2015 with a dedicated and highly experienced management team to provide industries with the best-fit packaging for their needs. The company primarily served the Lebanese and Moroccan markets with flexible packaging films, providing its customers with the best consultation services along with a competitive advantage in the field.

As of 2017, TETRAX provided services to more than 20 African countries and had a permanent sales presence in both East and West Africa.

In 2020, the company relocated its head offices to Dubai, UAE, and began expanding its services to include machine consultancy and supply, in addition to turnkey projects, with the assistance of a professional team of engineers.

By 2022, TETRAX had identified a need to assist several African countries in exporting their products, when another unit was created to approach and make liaisons with global markets in terms of sales and logistics executing and facilitating the export process. TETRAX will look for every opportunity to contribute to, develop, and improve the communities in which it operates in its countries of presence.
