RIPENING ROOMS

TETRAX specializes in providing ripening room preparation services for other businesses that deal with fresh produce. We understand that ripening is a critical step in the post-harvest process and can significantly impact the quality and shelf life of fruits and vegetables. As such, we offer customized solutions tailored to the specific needs of our clients. Our experienced team works closely with our clients to understand their requirements and design ripening rooms that meet their unique needs. We use high-quality equipment and technology to ensure precise control of temperature, humidity, and ethylene levels to create the optimal environment for maturation. Our goal is to provide our clients with ripening rooms that deliver the highest quality produce that meets or exceeds their customers' expectations.
